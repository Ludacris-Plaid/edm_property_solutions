The Opportunity Score

Our proprietary scoring blends pricing efficiency, rental yield, location quality, condition, and local growth potential into a single 1–5 rating. It helps quickly prioritize properties with the best upside and fit.

View Featured Properties
Scoring Signals
  • Price efficiency vs. local range
  • Rental yield relative to market maximum
  • Location quality (schools, transport, amenities)
  • Property condition and work required
  • Neighborhood growth potential
  • Bonuses for below‑market pricing and time on market
1–5 Opportunity Score

We normalize inputs to 0–1, combine them with tuned weights, apply strategic bonuses, and map the result to an intuitive 1–5 rating.
